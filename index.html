Bio

Yinka Olowe is an independent researcher specializing in machine learning and deep learning, with a strong focus on the development of intelligent systems. His interdisciplinary research spans human-computer interaction, cognitive science, computational modeling, and AI in education. Currently, he is working on innovative intelligent tutoring and learning systems that aim to enhance educational outcomes.

In addition to his independent research, Yinka serves as a research associate for Seedbuilders Foundation, an NGO dedicated to providing access to education for internally displaced persons (IDP) affected by Boko Haram terrorism in northern Nigeria. His work at Seedbuilders Foundation is pivotal in creating educational opportunities and fostering hope for communities in crisis.
