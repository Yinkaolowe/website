Bio

I am an independent researcher specializing in machine learning and deep learning, and I focus on developing intelligent systems for diverse fields. My interdisciplinary research spans human-computer interaction, cognitive systems, computational modeling, and AI in education. I am also the technical product manager at  Ulesson - an ed-tech startup, where I am currently working on an Advanced intelligent tutoring system that aims to enhance educational outcomes.

In addition to my independent research, I serve as a research associate for Seedbuilders Foundation, an NGO dedicated to providing access to education for internally displaced people (IDP) affected by Boko Haram terrorism in northern Nigeria. My work at Seedbuilders Foundation is pivotal in creating educational opportunities with AI technology and fostering hope for communities in crisis.

I love running, playing soccer and debating about the Singularity.
